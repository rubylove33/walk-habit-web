Privacy Policy

Walk Habit (hereinafter referred to as "App") values your privacy and complies with applicable laws and regulations. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and stores your personal information.

1. Privacy Principles

The App can be used without a separate sign-up process, and it uses minimal device permissions solely for providing services. As a rule, the developer does not transmit or collect personal identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.) to any external server.

2. Information We Collect and Purpose of Use

The permissions requested by the App and their purposes are as follows:

  • Camera & Photo Library Permissions: For taking workout verification photos, checking for faces (privacy warning), storing body check records, and applying templates. Face detection is performed entirely on-device.
  • Payment Information (In-App Purchase): For purchasing and restoring Premium Membership. Payments are processed via Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • App Tracking Transparency (iOS): Providing personalized advertisements via IDFA and measuring ad effectiveness.

3. Retention and Destruction of Data

  • All data in the App is stored only on your device.
  • Biometric authentication (Face ID/Fingerprint) signal is handled by the OS; the App never accesses the biometric data itself.
  • Deleting the App or using "Clear Data" erases all data immediately.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We use Google AdMob for advertisements and Firebase Crashlytics for stability analysis. Data processed includes Device Identifiers, Product Interaction, and Crash Data.

5. Privacy Rights

Users have rights under GDPR (EU) and CCPA/CPRA (California) to access, delete, or opt-out of data processing/tracking through device settings.
